The Details

Everyone loves deep-v shirts. They are stylish and will always make you look great. But with the chest exposed, the neck region has to suffer from heat loss.

To combat this, we worked many long hours with our team of designers to develop CNW™: Comfort Neck Warming technology. CNW will insulate your neck region while simultaneously leaving the chest area open allowing it to breathe. CNW is breathable and reinforced for superior mobility.
